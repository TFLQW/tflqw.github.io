Robotics Researcher

Harbin Institute of Technology

State Key Laboratory of Robotics and System

I am a doctor candidate of mechanical engineering supervised by Jie Zhao at the state key laboratory of robotics and system in Harbin Institute of Technology, and I was a research intern supervised by Sylvain Calinon in Idiap Research Institute from 2021 to 2022. My research interests include robot skill learning, learning from demonstration and imitation learning. I am also interested in optimal control and geometry.
