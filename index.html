Robotics Researcher

Shenyang Institute of Automation

State Key Laboratory of Robotics

I am a Assistant Researcher in Shenyang Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, SIA. I am a Ph.D. of mechanical engineering supervised by Jie Zhao at the state key laboratory of robotics and systems in Harbin Institute of Technology from 2017 to 2024, and I was also a research intern supervised by Sylvain Calinon in Idiap Research Institute from 2021 to 2022. My research interests include robot skill learning, learning from demonstration and imitation learning. I am also interested in optimal control and bimanual manipulation.
